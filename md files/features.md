# Feature Specification: Atlas AI

**Status:** Draft
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-09

This document outlines the planned features for the **Atlas AI** Career Guidance Platform, categorized by function. It serves as a master checklist for the development roadmap.

## 1. Core Platform & Unified Profile
- [ ] **Unified AI-Native Portfolio Profile**: 
    - **Hybrid Architecture**: Merges **LinkedIn-style** professional narrative (experience, education) with **GitHub-style** proof of work (project codebases, activity heatmaps, live demos).
    - **Dynamic Portfolio Resume**: The profile acts as a live, shareable URL that doubles as a high-fidelity web portfolio and an exportable PDF resume.
    - **Intelligent Profiling Engine**: AI-driven profile creation from academic records, imported resumes, and GitHub/LinkedIn authentications.
    - **Proof-of-Skill Badges**: Integration with the Gamification layer and Skill Verification system.
- [ ] **Data Privacy & Ethics Hub**: 
    - GDPR/FERPA-compliant data handling.
    - Transparent AI disclosure (explaining how the profile is being analyzed).
- [ ] **Onboarding Wizard**: 
    - Gamified initial assessment to establish baseline interests and skills.

## 2. Career Discovery & Exploration
- [ ] **AI Career Recommendation Engine**: 
    - Suggests 3-5 top career paths based on suitability (skills match), feasibility (market demand), and passion (user interest).
    - Uses Hybrid Filtering (Content + Collaborative).
- [ ] **Interactive Career Maps**: 
    - Visual "skill trees" showing progression from Student -> Junior Role -> Senior Role.
- [ ] **Role "Test Drive" Simulations**: 
    - Short interactive scenarios or "day in the life" content for specific careers.

## 3. Skill Intelligence & Gap Analysis
- [ ] **Skill Gap Visualizer**: 
    - Radar charts comparing "My Skills" vs. "Required Skills" for target roles.
- [ ] **Personalized Learning Pathways**: 
    - Curated list of courses (MOOCs, internal electives) to bridge identified gaps.
- [ ] **Skill Verification System**: 
    - Integration with assessments or project uploads to validate claimed skills.

## 4. Academic Alignment
- [ ] **Coursework Relevance Mapper**: 
    - Explains *why* a current college subject matters for the chosen career ("You need Database Management for Data Analyst roles").
- [ ] **Elective Recommendation System**: 
    - AI suggestions for university electives that boost employability in target fields.
- [ ] **Project Ideas Generator**: 
    - Suggests capstone projects that align with career goals and current skill gaps.

## 5. Experience & Job Readiness
- [ ] **Smart Resume Builder**: 
    - AI-powered content suggestions and formatting checks against Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).
- [ ] **Mock Interview Simulator**: 
    - AI chatbot conducting role-specific technical and behavioral interviews with feedback.
- [ ] **Internship Matcher**: 
    - Recommendation of relevant internships based on readiness score.

## 6. Decision Support & Analytics
- [ ] **Career Comparator Tool**: 
    - Side-by-side comparison of multiple career paths (Salary, Growth, Work-Life Balance).
- [ ] **"Future Self" Projector**: 
    - Simulations of career trajectories 5, 10, and 15 years into the future.
- [ ] ** pivot Simulator**: 
    - "What if I switch to Marketing?" analysis showing transferable skills and new gaps.

## 7. Trust & Transparency (XAI)
- [ ] **"Why This?" Explainer**: 
    - Determining factors for every AI recommendation (e.g., "Recommended because you excel in Math and enjoy solving puzzles").
- [ ] **Confidence Scores**: 
    - Displaying AI confidence levels (Low/Medium/High) for predictions.
- [ ] **Feedback Loop**: 
    - "Was this helpful?" mechanisms to refine individual and global models.

## 8. Gamification & Engagement (New)
- [ ] **Career Journey Quests**:
    - Milestone-based missions (e.g., "Complete your first Skill Gap analysis") with rewards.
- [ ] **Atlas Badges & Levels**:
    - Digital badges for certifications, soft skill mastery, and platform consistency.
    - Levelling system from "Novice Explorer" to "Career Master."
- [ ] **Virtual Skill Challenges**:
    - Weekly AI-driven mini-games that test aptitude in specific fields.

## 9. Social Learning & Mentorship (New)
- [ ] **Alumni Smart-Matching**:
    - Connects current students with alumni in their target fields for informal 1:1 mentorship.
- [ ] **Peer Collaboration Hub**:
    - Community forums and project rooms where students can work on cross-disciplinary projects.
- [ ] **Expert Office Hours**:
    - Scheduled live sessions or asynchronous Q&A with industry experts.

## 10. Future-Proofing & Emerging Tech (New)
- [ ] **Agentic Job Assistant**:
    - An AI agent that can autonomously search for and suggest tailored job/internship openings based on your profile.
- [ ] **VR Office Previews**:
    - Immersive 360-degree experiences of corporate environments and "day-in-the-life" workplace simulations.
- [ ] **Skill Credentialing on Blockchain**:
    - Verified, immutable digital certificates for skills earned through the platform.
- [ ] **Well-being & Burnout Monitoring**:
    - AI that monitors student engagement and flags potential burnout or anxiety, suggesting breaks or mental health resources.

## 11. Market Intelligence & Financial Support (Core - Promoted)
- [ ] **Industry Trend Radar**:
    - Real-time dashboard of trending skills with career "weather forecast" (Growing/Stable/Declining).
    - Historical trends analysis and emerging job role alerts.
    - Time-series forecasting on job posting data.
- [ ] **Financial Aid & Scholarship Finder**:
    - AI matches students to scholarships, grants, and financial aid opportunities.
    - Application deadline reminders and essay writing assistance.
    - Removes financial barriers to education and skill development.

## 12. Student Success & Career Readiness (Core - High-Demand Features)
- [ ] **Soft Skills Bootcamp with AI Role-Play**:
    - AI-powered simulations for difficult conversations, conflict resolution, and professional communication.
    - Real-time feedback on tone, body language (via webcam), and word choice.
    - Gamified progression with certification upon completion.
    - Addresses the critical soft skills gap (82% of managers report Gen Z needs support).
- [ ] **Career Clarity Coach (24/7 AI Counselor)**:
    - GPT-4 powered 1:1 career counselor with long-term memory.
    - Deep conversations for career decision-making and interview feedback.
    - Escalates to human counselor when needed.
    - Solves the 376:1 student-to-counselor ratio problem.
- [ ] **AI-Powered Experience Translator**:
    - Translates non-traditional experience (side hustles, YouTube, volunteering) into professional skills.
    - Generates resume bullet points with professional phrasing.
    - Suggests relevant job roles where unconventional skills apply.
- [ ] **Ghost Job Detector & Scam Shield**:
    - AI scans job postings for red flags (unrealistic salary, vague descriptions, unverified companies).
    - Trust Score (0-100) for each job posting with community reporting.
    - Protects students from fake jobs (71% of Gen Z encounter ghost jobs).
- [ ] **Side Hustle Incubator**:
    - Helps students start career-aligned side hustles (design, web dev, marketing).
    - Step-by-step guides for gig platform setup, pricing, and client acquisition.
    - Income tracker and automatic portfolio building.
    - Addresses financial stress (74% of students struggle financially).
