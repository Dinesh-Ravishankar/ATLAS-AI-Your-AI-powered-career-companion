# Research & Discoveries

## Discovery Phase
- **North Star:** "Your AI-Powered Career Companion from Confusion to Clarity" - End-to-end platform transforming scattered career aspirations into actionable roadmaps.
- **Integrations:** GitHub (Projects/Heatmaps), ESCO/O*NET (Skills), Job Market APIs, University Databases, Course Aggregators.
- **Source of Truth:** Atlas Card (Unified Portfolio Profile acting as resume, portfolio, and data store).
- **Delivery Payload:** Interactive Web Application + Atlas AI Chatbot (conversational orchestrator).
- **Behavioral Rules:** Ethical, private, explainable, proactive, and contextual.
