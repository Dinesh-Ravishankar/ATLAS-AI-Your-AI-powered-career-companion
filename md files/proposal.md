# Project Proposal: ATLAS AI

## Title
**ATLAS AI – An Intelligent Career Guidance and Decision Support Platform for Pre-College and College Students**

## 1. Introduction
ATLAS AI is an AI-powered, unified career guidance platform designed to support students from pre-college entry through college and early career preparation. It provides personalized, explainable, and continuous career assistance, leveraging AI/ML to bridge the gap between education and employability.

## 2. Problem Statement
Students face challenges in making informed career decisions due to:
- Lack of awareness of diverse career paths.
- Poor alignment between academic learning and industry requirements.
- Fragmented platforms for jobs, skills, and guidance.
- Late-stage intervention and over-reliance on biased informal sources.

## 3. Objectives
- Provide continuous and personalized career guidance.
- Discover suitable career paths based on interests, skills, and aptitude.
- Analyze skill gaps and recommend focused learning roadmaps.
- Align academic choices with career goals.
- Assist in internship, project, resume, and interview preparation.
- Support informed decision-making via AI insights.

## 4. Proposed System – ATLAS AI
ATLAS AI centers on a **Unified User Profile** (hybrid LinkedIn/GitHub) storing academic details, skills, projects, and interests. 
- **Core Interface:** Integrated AI chatbot (conversational orchestrator).
- **Architecture:** 5-Layered system (UI, Orchestration, AI Intelligence, Knowledge, Data/Profile).

## 5. Expected Outcomes
- A complete, production-ready platform.
- Full suite of integrated career features.
- End-to-end support via a conversational AI bot.
